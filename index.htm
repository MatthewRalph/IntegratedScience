The Colorado Rockies were founded in 1991 and first played in 1993. The team, as of yet, has never won a World Series. However, in 2007, the Rockies went to the World Series but lost to the Boston Red Sox. You can find more information on the Colorado Rockies, when they play next, and how to get ticketshere.


The Boston Red Sox were founded in 1901. Since then, the Red Sox have won nine World Series, the latest being in 2018. Their homefield, Fenway Park, is the oldest stadium in Major League history. You can find more information on the Boston Red Sox, when they play next, and how to get ticketshere.


The New York Yankees were originally founded in Baltimore on January 9, 1903. It was purchased that same year and moved to New York City. The Yankees were originally called the New York Highlanders up until 1913 when the name was officially changed. The team holds the most World Series wins at twenty-seven.
